| Operation | Config | Time | Result |

## Key observations

- **Build time scales as O(N³ + M·N²)** — dominated by eigh at N≥200
- **Predict time O(N·M)** — microseconds even for N=500, M=100 (vs 177ms for exact eigh)
- **Inverse design** converges in ~670ms for 30 steps — adaptive W refresh avoids unnecessary eigh
- **Graph adapter** uses `edge_laplacian` basis — avoids materialising ~306.1MB of edge basis matrices
- **Monodromy** is O(|loop|·N³) — unavoidable, every point needs eigh
- **Hessian FD** is O(M·N³) — 118ms for M=10, scales linearly with M
